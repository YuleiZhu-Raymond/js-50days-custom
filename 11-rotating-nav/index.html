Zero Two - Darling in the Franxx

Code: 002

Zero Two is the mysterious and elite pilot from the anime Darling in the Franxx. Known by her codename 002, she possesses pink hair, red horns, and a striking presence. Dubbed the "Partner Killer", her co-pilots rarely survive more than three rides. Her FRANXX unit is Strelizia, which she pilots with Hiro (Code: 016), whom she affectionately calls "Darling".

Personality & Traits

  • Energetic, teasing, and sweet-toothed — often seen with lollipops
  • Strong and agile in combat
  • Emotionally complex, growing from cold to compassionate
  • Highly protective of Hiro, evolving from possessiveness to self-sacrifice

Visual Identity

Her design features pink hair, green eyes, and a red uniform. In battle, she dons a tight red suit. As her Klaxosaur side awakens, physical traits like fangs and claws begin to show. Her FRANXX, Strelizia, also reflects red-white-blue colors resembling a mecha-princess motif.
